Who We Are


We design affordable, 3D-printed prosthetic limbs for athletes in partnership with Boston Dynamics. We also provide mental health support and insurance options to improve accessibility and care.


Our Mission


To help athletes with limb loss regain confidence in movement, training, and competition through accessible, high-quality affordable 3D-printed prosthetics.


Get Started By


Creating a free account, scan your limb with your phone, and upload it to generate your personalized dashboard.


Step 1: Sign Up or Log In


Create an account, scan your limb, and access your dashboard.


Step 2: Open the Design Tab


Click the pencil icon in the top-left corner of your dashboard.


Step 3: Start a New Design


Select “New Design” to open customization options.


Step 4: Choose Your Prosthetic


Select a prosthetic and customize material, color, and fit.


Step 5: Place Your Order


Click “Order” at the top, complete the form, and choose a nearby FabLab.

Nylon (Polyamide / PA12)


Very popular for sports prosthetics. It’s strong, lightweight, and slightly flexible—great for absorbing impact during running or jumping.


Carbon Fiber–Reinforced Polymers


Used when you need maximum strength and energy return (like sprinting blades). Carbon fiber adds stiffness and durability while keeping weight low.


PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol)


More flexible and impact-resistant than PLA. Good for parts that need durability without being too rigid.


TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane)


A rubber-like material used for flexible components like liners, grips, or shock-absorbing areas.
