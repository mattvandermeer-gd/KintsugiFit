Who We Are


A company in partnership with Boston Dynamics that specializes in providing cost effective 3D printed prosthetic limbs for athletes who have lost a limb. Also providing mental health support and insurance options for purchasing a prosthetic.


Our Mission


The goal we are trying to achieve for athletes who have lost a limb to gain their confidence back in exercising and competing. We also want to provide affordable and accessible prosthetics to all athletes.


Get Started By


Sign up with a free account. You can put in your information about your limb measurments and preferences. You can also scan your limb with your phone and upload that.


Step 1: Scan Limb with Phone


Scan your limb with your phone and we will generate a personal dashboard from your data.


Step 2: Go to the Design Tab


This is the pencil icon on the top bar.


Step 3: Click on New Design


Click on the “New Design” icon to open up the customization page.


Step 4: Pick Out Your Prosthetic


Select the type of prosthetic, select the variant, change the values, select colors, and select materials.


Step 5: When You Are Ready, Place An Order


Find the "Order Design" button and click on it to go to the order page.

Nylon (Polyamide / PA12)


It is a tough material and has carbon strands which make it easy to reshape if needed.



Carbon fiber reinforced (CFR)


This material is rigid and is lightweight. Offers great optimization for the user.



Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU)


This material allows for more flexibility and comfort in prosthetics. It can be used by molding it into complex shapes.
