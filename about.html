ABOUT

Who We Are

A company in partnership with Boston Dynamics that specializes in providing cost effective 3D printed prosthetic limbs for athletes who have lost a limb. Also providing mental health support and insurance options for purchasing a prosthetic.

Mission

The goal we are trying to achieve for athletes who have lost a limb to gain their confidence back in exercising and competing. We also want to provide affordable and accessible prosthetics to all athletes.

How To Get Started

Sign up with a free account. You can put in your information about your limb measurments and preferences. You can also scan your limb with your phone and upload that.
